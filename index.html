Research

I’ve been working in the University of Michigan Crowds + Machines Lab since freshman year. Currently, I’m working on the Hybrid Teams project in the mental models cluster. Our goal, at a high level, is to research the effects of introducing AI agents with varied ML models into a Human/AI team, given some classification task, in order to find out if the human worker is capable of maintaining an adequate mental model of their AI teammates or master the given classification task.
